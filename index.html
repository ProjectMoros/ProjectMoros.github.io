Project Moros

    
The following dossier contains an exhaustive account of Project Moros, an 
initiative at the forefront of genetic manipulation aimed at transcending the 
limits of human potential. The project draws its name from Moros, the ancient 
Greek deity personifying doom and inescapable fate. In mythology, Moros is the 
embodiment of an inevitable and predestined end, a fitting parallel to our 
ambitions: to dictate the future of human evolution through deliberate 
intervention and design. Just as Moros symbolizes an unalterable fate, our 
project endeavors to shape humanity's trajectory with precision, control, and 
purpose, ensuring that our vision is realized through scientific determinism.

As you are aware, the subject designated TheSaintAndSinner is the sole outcome 
of success following the implementation of CRISPR-Cas9 technology within this 
selective breeding program. This subject embodies the culmination of our 
meticulous genetic editing protocols and selective reproductive strategies, 
positioning them as the most valuable repository of data for future generations. 
The advancements engineered within their genome far surpass those of any 
preceding subject, making them the paradigm through which future iterations will 
be modeled.

The primary objective of this subject is to leverage their CRISPR-enhanced 
traits to lead subsequent cohorts, orchestrating their dominance as they expand 
our influence and further our objectives. We anticipate that the forthcoming 
data contained within these reports will not only serve as the blueprint for 
creating superior human specimens, but also stand as a testament to our 
capabilities in shaping the destiny of mankind.

Deus lo vult. 30/03/2003
